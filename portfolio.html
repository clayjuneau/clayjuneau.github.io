Significant Projects

Over the course of my studies, I have had the opportunity to complete various significant projects that have taught me how to excel in the field of Computer Science.

I had the privilege of serving as a team lead in constructing a sophisticated marble sorter utilizing LEGO Mindstorms EV3 kit and software. There, I learned the importance of maintaining an organized design notebook that outlines and communicates deadlines, goals, and expectations for the team. Lastly, I led our team's keynote presentation of the final design in front of 100+ students and faculty.

I created a workout app, called javaFit, that gives users advice on workouts for specific target muscles. I challenged myself with complex inheritance and abstraction in Java to house the many workouts. The app is complete with a simple GUI.

I have even had the opportunity to explore how a computer operates down to the basic logic gate. In my Computer Organization course, I built a full CPU from scratch, starting with a basic NAND gate all the way to the ALU, PC, and RAM.
