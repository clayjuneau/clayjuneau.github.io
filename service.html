Service



I have been involved in the Brotherhood of Christian Aggies since my freshman year of college in 2017. BCA service revolves around Gospel-centered ministries like "Save Our Streets" Ministries throughout the semester as well as seasonal mission trips with "Blueprint" Ministries in San Antonio, TX.





During the summer of 2018, I served as a overnight camp counselor at T Bar M Camps for high school campers for 7 weeks. I cultivated relationships in order to foster personal and spiritual growth in a fun, high-energy camp environment. In addition, I facilitated the campers on high ropes course, and I helped film and edit the weekly session video.
