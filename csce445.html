Assignment 2


Looking through my classmates' web pages, I noticed more similarities than differences in regards to the reasons we are in this class and the media that we consume.


Almost everyone is a Computer Science major or a technology-focused major that has an interest in computers. More specifically, there is a subgroup of students that this course attracts who are interested in design and the way humans interact with computers. Students are eager to learn about to ways technology and computers influence our daily consumption of information.


I also noticed common trends related to the types of media students consume. Because this class contains students who are roughly the same age and have similar academic interests, there are many overlapping similarities in the forms of media they use. Almost everyone uses their phone to message people via text messaging and GroupMe, a common application for students to communicate about classes. The students, Aashish Ananthanarayan, Lina Zhang, Jason Kolodziej, Allison Reuthinger, and Emily Davis, all frequently use Instagram and other forms of social media. Lastly, almost everyone in the class plays at least one type of video game, whether it’s a mobile game, console game, or computer game.
